The success of TR-MISR\cite{TR-MISR-original} on the PROBA-V satellite benchmark, achieving the highest corrected PSNR and SSIM 
among multi-image SR methods, was main motivation of this work: that replacing the ConvGRU fusion stage with a Transformer could 
improve both reconstruction quality and inference speed for SOFI microscopy. 
The satellite and microscopy tasks share the same fundamental structure: $N$ misaligned, 
noise-corrupted low-resolution frames must be fused into a single high-resolution image. 
Several properties of the Transformer made it a compelling candidate for this domain transfer.
